Hello

I'm Alison Benjamin. I'm a Web developer working for the Frontline Club and its projects, like the Frontline Freelance Register.

I'm also interested in data visualisation with D3.JS and a have few side projects, which you can read about on my blog.

I hold a Masters from the University of Toronto (Faculty of Library and Information Science, 2010). I have also worked with the Inclusive Design Research Center at OCAD University and in the Faculty of Computer Science at the University of Toronto in the UX and accessibility space.
